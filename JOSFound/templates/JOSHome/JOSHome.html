Participatory Entertainment for Healthier Minds



JoinOurStory.com is a California benefit corporation that offers subscribers, especially seniors, an enriching recreational activity: playing a role in an ongoing collaborative drama.

Everyone in the community will have the chance to create their own character and influence the action - with personal coaching available.

The plot regularly advances through professional videos and related media, starring employee-owners and select subscribers. Through this adventure, JoinOurStory.com rewards performers for sharing their talent and gives seniors, their friends and their families a taste of show business magic in order to promote happy souls and healthy minds.

Email info@joinourstory.com for more information.

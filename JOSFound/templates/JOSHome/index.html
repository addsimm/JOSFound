Participatory Entertainment for Healthier Minds







JoinOurStory.com is a California benefit corporation that offers subscribers, especially seniors, an irresistibly fun activity — taking part in an ongoing drama!

Every member has the opportunity to create a charter and influence the action, with patient coaching available.

High quality videos, starring professional actors and select subscribers regularly transform the contributions into a powerfully engaging soap opera. Together with an immersive 24/7 online world and exhilarating live performances, JoinOurStory.com draws subscribers into a thriving, creative community, rewarding performers for sharing their talent and giving subscribers a taste of show business magic in order to foster happy souls and healthy minds.
